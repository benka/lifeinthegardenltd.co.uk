Gardening & Landscaping

Gardening

Spending your free time in a beautiful garden is a great way to relax and recharge, but keeping it clean and well shaped can be time-consuming. We are here to help should you need a complete overhaul or just an extra pair of hands to bring harmony to your garden.

Garden Maintenance

We provide friendly, efficient regular garden maintenance to both residential and commercial customers, weekly, fortnightly and monthly or just one off tidy ups.

Garden clearence: we can help you to get your overgrown garden back in control. This service available at any time of the year either in stages or all at once, depending on your preferences.

Weed control: we can help you to keep patios, paths, beds, borders and pots clear of any weeds, moss, algies.

Planting: we supply plants for your requirements. Before planting we always make sure the ground well prepared, the soil enriched and improved using the appropriate method.

Pots and planters: we design seasonal displays or we can create and maintaine your balcony or terrace by following your instructions.

Pruning and hedge trimming: keeping plants healthy and strong. We prune fruit trees, roses, controll climbers and shape hedges and bushes.

Plant disase identification and controll: if you are not sure what is affecting your plants. We can help you to diagnose the problems. We will also provide both organic and chemical treatments as well as advice to help prevent problems in the future.

Watering: we can turn up regularly and water your pots on your balcony or water your lawn or plants. We can also design, supply and install your automatic watering system.

Jet washing & patio cleaning: we provide safe and professional jet washing for your driveway, patio and garden paths. In order to deliver the pressure washing service we will require access to a tap with decent water pressure, a power point and drainage in place for the water to flow away from the site.

Leaf clearing: it is an important job to clear away autumn leaves from your garden year to year.

Garden Clearance

Our garden clearance service is for areas that need to be cleared for landscaping or other construction, for neglected or overgrown gardens.

We break up and remove patios, dismantle sheds, take apart concrete paths, demolish garden walks, etc. We also clear any kind of weeds, bushes, plants, trees, old turf or trunks and roots. We can clear areas quickly and efficiently, disposing of the green waste into licenced recycling centres.

The work is quicker if there is direct access to the garden. Otherwise the waste from the garden will need to be carried through the property. In these cases we cover your floor and protect walks and furniture. We will of course clean the area before the job is finished and keep disruption to a minimum.

Garden clearance waste can be bagged up and left on the site for council collection. Or we can take it straight for an additional charge. For waste disposal informations please see our price list below. With large volumes it could be necessary to hire a skip or a lorry. We arrange waste disposal, skip hire and road permit for you if required.

For smaller garden clearance projects we are able to discuss and quote the job on phone. We suggest to arrange a site meeting if the job takes longer than one or two days or need more worker than one or two.
